1.0 About Us

Who we are

Hiltop Research & Consultancy provides project management, strategic research and advisory services on sustainable projects in PNG’s renewable and non‑renewable natural resource sectors. We deliver environmental and socio‑economic advisory, partnering with reputable local and international consulting and engineering firms to meet clients' expectation.

  • Addressing environmental and socio‑economic issues in resource development projects.
  • Providing strategic advisory, project design, management and implementation.
  • Sector focus: forestry, agriculture, fisheries, renewable energy, mining, gas & petroleum, and urban & rural infrastructure.

Experience & Capabilities

Our multi‑disciplinary team brings decades of experience in natural resources management and project planning. We have delivered baseline studies, feasibility assessments, EIAs, SIAs, CEMP and permit processes across PNG.
